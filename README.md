## 🎮 How to Play

### 2 Player Mode
1. Click **👥 2 Player** button
2. Player X goes first
3. Click any empty cell to place your mark
4. First to get 3 in a row wins!

### vs AI Mode
1. Click **🤖 vs AI (Hard)** button
2. You play as **X**, AI plays as **O**
3. Click any empty cell
4. AI will respond with its best move
5. Try to win — if you can! 😎
